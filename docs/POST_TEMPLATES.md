# Post Templates

Use these as reusable starters. Keep voice natural and edit before posting.

---

## Sticker Quest - Parent Community Template

Title idea:
"I built this for my niece starting Kindergarten - would parents test this?"

Body template:

"I am building Sticker Quest as a family-first learning app for Kindergarten.
It ties simple phonics/movement tasks to a short daily routine.

I built this because I wanted something that felt safer and calmer than typical screen time.
No ads, no tracking, and parent controls stay central.

Feedback from parents/teachers is always welcome: [feedback link]

AI note (for transparency): I am a solo builder with coding gaps, so AI helps me prototype and keep shipping. Final child-facing decisions, edits, and testing are mine. As budget allows, I plan to bring in human collaborators for art/audio/polish."

Ask-for-feedback prompt:
"What would make you trust this more as a parent before trying it?"

## The Anchor's Desk - Indie Dev Template

Title idea:
"Text-noir roguelike concept: redact one word, reality changes"

Body template:

"I am prototyping a text-noir roguelike where you play as an editor.
Each turn: one document, one redaction choice, one consequence.

Core hook:
Redact one word, reality changes.

I am looking for feedback on readability, pacing, and whether the loop feels interesting.
Feedback channel: [feedback link]

AI note (for transparency): AI helps me bridge technical gaps as a solo creator, but final creative direction and release decisions are mine. As budget allows, I plan to work with human collaborators where they add the most value."

Ask-for-feedback prompt:
"Which part of this hook feels strongest, and what feels unclear?"
