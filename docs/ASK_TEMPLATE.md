# Ask Template (When You Are Not Sure How To Ask)

Use any of these starters in chat. Copy/paste and fill the blanks.

## Quick Starters

1. "I do not know the right term, but I want [outcome]. What is the best way to do that?"
2. "What am I missing here that an experienced builder would check?"
3. "Give me safest / fastest / smartest options for [problem]. Recommend one."
4. "Explain this like I am brand new, then give me 3 steps."
5. "I feel stuck. What should I do next in the next 30 minutes?"

## Planning Starters

1. "What should I focus on this week to keep launch on track?"
2. "What are my top 3 risks right now and how do I reduce them?"
3. "If I only have [X] hours this week, what should I do first?"
4. "What can I postpone safely without hurting launch?"

## Debugging Starter

"The app is [symptom]. I am not sure what caused it. Give me the fastest check path and tell me what to report back."

## Confidence Starter

"How confident are you in this recommendation (1-10), and what would raise confidence?"
